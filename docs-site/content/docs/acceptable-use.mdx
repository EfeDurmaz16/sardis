## 1. Overview

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of Sardis payment infrastructure for AI agents. Violation of this policy may result in immediate suspension or termination of your account.

## 2. Permitted Uses

Sardis is designed for legitimate AI agent payment operations, including:

- AI agents paying for API services (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)
- Automated procurement of digital goods and services
- Agent-to-agent transactions for collaborative tasks
- Subscription and billing management for AI workflows
- Development, testing, and integration of payment features
- Research and educational purposes in AI economics

## 3. Prohibited Activities

### Illegal Activities
Money laundering, terrorist financing, sanctions evasion, payments for illegal goods, human trafficking, child exploitation, or any activity that violates applicable laws.

### Fraud and Deception
Identity fraud, phishing, unauthorized access to wallets, market manipulation, fake merchant descriptions, or circumventing compliance controls.

### Harmful Content and Services
Payment for malware, DDoS attacks, disinformation campaigns, harassment services, or non-consensual intimate imagery.

### Restricted Financial Activities
Unlicensed money transmission, pyramid schemes, unlicensed securities offerings, or unlicensed gambling.

### System Abuse
Bypassing rate limits, reverse engineering, interfering with other users, stress testing without permission, or automated account creation to circumvent limits.

## 4. AI Agent Responsibilities

When using Sardis with AI agents, you are responsible for:

- **Policy Configuration:** Setting appropriate spending limits and category restrictions
- **Monitoring:** Reviewing agent transactions for anomalies
- **Human Oversight:** Maintaining ability to pause or revoke agent permissions
- **Prompt Safety:** Ensuring agent prompts do not encourage policy violations
- **Logging:** Maintaining audit trails for agent decision-making

> **Important:** You are legally responsible for all transactions executed by your AI agents. "The AI did it" is not a valid defense for policy violations or illegal activity.

## 5. Enforcement

Violations may result in warning (minor, first-time violations), temporary suspension (pending investigation), permanent termination (serious or repeated violations), fund freezing (where required by law), or legal action.
