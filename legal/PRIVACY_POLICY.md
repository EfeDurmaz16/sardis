# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 25, 2026

Sardis ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our payment infrastructure services.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Email address, name, and contact details when you create an account
- **Identity Verification:** Government-issued ID, address verification, and other KYC documentation (processed through Persona)
- **Wallet Information:** Public wallet addresses (we never have access to private keys)
- **Transaction Details:** Payment amounts, recipients, and purposes
- **Policy Configurations:** Spending rules and restrictions you define

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data:** API calls, tool usage, and interaction patterns
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, and operating system
- **Transaction Metadata:** Timestamps, chain IDs, and transaction hashes

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties

- **KYC Providers:** Identity verification results from Persona
- **AML Screening:** Risk scores and compliance flags from Elliptic
- **Card Networks:** Transaction data from Lithic for virtual card usage
- **Banking Partners:** ACH and wire transfer status from Bridge

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide and maintain our payment services
- Process transactions and enforce spending policies
- Verify your identity and comply with legal requirements
- Detect and prevent fraud, money laundering, and unauthorized access
- Communicate service updates and support responses
- Improve our services through analytics (anonymized)
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

- **Contract Performance:** To provide services you've requested
- **Legal Obligation:** To comply with KYC/AML regulations
- **Legitimate Interest:** To prevent fraud and improve services
- **Consent:** For optional features and marketing communications

## 4. Information Sharing

We share information with:

### 4.1 Service Providers
- **Turnkey:** MPC wallet infrastructure (key shards only, never full keys)
- **Persona:** Identity verification
- **Elliptic:** AML/sanctions screening
- **Lithic:** Virtual card issuance
- **Bridge:** Fiat on/off-ramp services

### 4.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when required by law, subpoena, or government request.

### 4.3 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be transferred.

### 4.4 Never Sold
**We never sell your personal information to third parties.**

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **MPC Architecture:** Private keys are split across multiple parties
- **Encryption:** TLS 1.3 for all data in transit; AES-256 for data at rest
- **Access Control:** Role-based access with audit logging
- **Infrastructure:** SOC 2 compliant cloud providers

## 6. Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active, deleted within 30 days of account closure
- **Transaction Records:** Retained for 7 years per regulatory requirements
- **KYC Documents:** Retained for 5 years after account closure per AML regulations
- **Usage Analytics:** Anonymized and aggregated after 90 days

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 For All Users
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request data deletion (subject to legal retention requirements)
- Export your data in a portable format
- Opt out of marketing communications

### 7.2 Additional Rights (EU/UK - GDPR)
- Object to processing based on legitimate interest
- Restrict processing
- Withdraw consent at any time
- Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

### 7.3 Additional Rights (California - CCPA)
- Know what personal information is collected
- Request deletion of personal information
- Opt out of sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
- Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

## 8. International Data Transfers

We may transfer data to the United States and other countries. We use:

- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
- Data Processing Agreements with all third-party providers
- Adequate safeguards as required by GDPR

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately.

## 10. Cookies and Tracking

Our web interfaces use:

- **Essential Cookies:** Required for service functionality
- **Analytics Cookies:** To understand usage patterns (can be disabled)
- **No Advertising Cookies:** We do not use tracking for advertising
