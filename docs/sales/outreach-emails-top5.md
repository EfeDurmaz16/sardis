## DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT (15 minutes)

If they agree to a call, follow this structure:

**Minutes 1-2: Rapport**
"Thanks for making time. Before I get into anything, I'd love to hear — what's [Company] focused on right now?"

**Minutes 3-7: Situation + Problem (SPIN)**
- "How do your agents currently handle the payment step?"
- "What happens when an agent needs to complete a purchase but can't?"
- "Tell me about the last time an enterprise customer asked about spending controls."
- "How much engineering time have you spent on payment infrastructure?"

**Minutes 8-10: Implication**
- "What happens if you don't solve this in the next 6 months?"
- "Are you losing deals because of missing payment capability?"
- "What's your biggest competitor doing about this?"

**Minutes 11-13: Need-Payoff (light demo offer)**
- "If I could show you an agent making a real payment with policy controls in 2 minutes, would that be interesting?"
- "What would it mean for [Company] if your agents could spend money safely out of the box?"

**Minutes 14-15: Next Steps**
- "Would it make sense to do a 30-minute technical deep-dive where I show the SDK integration?"
- "Would you be open to a design partner agreement? No cost, just mutual commitment to explore."
