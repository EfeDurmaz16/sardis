## Executive Summary

This report documents the results of customer discovery interviews conducted to validate demand for Sardis' agent payment infrastructure. The goal is to answer:

## Interview Methodology

### Interview Script

**Opening:**
"Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time. I'm building Sardis, a payment infrastructure platform for AI agents. I'd love to understand your current payment challenges and see if we can help."

**Questions:**
1. **Current State:** How do agents/APIs handle payments today?
2. **Pain Points:** What are the biggest pain points with current methods?
3. **Willingness to Pay:** Would you pay for a solution? How much?
4. **Integration:** How complex would integration be?
5. **Timeline:** When would you need this? What's your timeline?
6. **Blockers:** What's preventing you from implementing payments today?
7. **Decision Process:** Who makes the decision? What's the process?

**Closing:**
"Thanks for your time. Would you be interested in being an early adopter? We're offering [incentive]."

## Validation Criteria

### Green Light (Proceed)
- ✅ 70%+ of interviews show strong demand
- ✅ Average willingness to pay > $100/month
- ✅ Average integration time < 2 weeks
- ✅ 3+ companies interested in early adoption
- ✅ Clear path to revenue within 3 months

### Yellow Light (Proceed with Caution)
- ⚠️ 50-70% of interviews show demand
- ⚠️ Average willingness to pay $50-100/month
- ⚠️ Average integration time 2-4 weeks
- ⚠️ 1-2 companies interested in early adoption
- ⚠️ Path to revenue unclear or > 6 months

### Red Light (Pivot or Delay)
- ❌ <50% of interviews show demand
- ❌ Average willingness to pay < $50/month
- ❌ Average integration time > 4 weeks
- ❌ No companies interested in early adoption
- ❌ No clear path to revenue
