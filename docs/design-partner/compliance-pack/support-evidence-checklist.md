# Support Channel Evidence Checklist

Date: 2026-03-04

## Required Support Surfaces

1. Public support email address.
2. In-app support entry point.
3. Dispute escalation channel with human ownership.

## Evidence Pack for Launch Review

1. Screenshots/links of support channels.
2. Complaint/dispute SOP version in force.
3. Sample receipt delivery proof.
4. Sample customer notification templates.
5. Incident escalation on-call mapping.

## Warm Integration Policy

1. `SARDIS_ISSUING_LIVE_ENABLED=false` in production until issuer approval.
2. Use sandbox keys and issuer sandbox endpoints for technical readiness.
3. Keep webhook validation/replay protections active in all environments.
