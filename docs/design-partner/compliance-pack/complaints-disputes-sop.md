# Complaints and Disputes SOP

Date: 2026-03-04

## Intake Requirements

Every support channel (dashboard, email, API support inbox) must support:

1. Complaint intake (dissatisfaction with product/service/policy).
2. Dispute/error intake (unauthorized charge or statement error).
3. Traceable ticket ID and timestamp.

## Operational Flow

1. Triage within SLA and classify as `complaint` or `dispute`.
2. Capture minimum facts: account, amount, transaction date, reason.
3. Escalate financial disputes immediately to issuer/provider workflow.
4. Preserve all communications and decisions for audit.
5. Track outcome and remediation.

## SLA and Ownership

1. First response SLA: same business day.
2. Dispute escalation owner: Payments Ops.
3. Final resolution owner: Compliance + Payments Ops.
4. Post-incident review for repeated root causes.

## Evidence

Retain:

1. Ticket timeline.
2. Agent/operator actions.
3. Provider submission IDs.
4. Final decision and customer notification proof.
