## Interview Script

### Opening (2 minutes)
"Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time. I'm [Your Name], founder of Sardis. We're building payment infrastructure for AI agents - think Stripe for agents, but non-custodial and AP2/TAP compliant. I'd love to understand your current payment challenges and see if we can help."

### Questions (20-30 minutes)

#### 1. Current State (5 minutes)
- How do your agents/APIs handle payments today?
- What payment methods do you use? (Credit cards, crypto, etc.)
- Who makes the payment decisions? (Human, agent, automated?)
- What's your current payment volume? (If comfortable sharing)

#### 2. Pain Points (5 minutes)
- What are the biggest pain points with current payment methods?
- What would you change if you could?
- Have you tried to solve this problem? How?
- What's preventing you from implementing agent payments today?

#### 3. Willingness to Pay (5 minutes)
- Would you pay for a solution that solves these problems?
- How much would you be willing to pay? (Monthly subscription, per-transaction fee, etc.)
- What would make it worth paying for?
- What's your budget for payment infrastructure?

#### 4. Integration (5 minutes)
- How complex would integration be for you?
- What's your technical team size?
- How long would integration take?
- What would you need from us? (SDK, documentation, support?)

#### 5. Timeline (5 minutes)
- When would you need this solution?
- What's your timeline for implementing payments?
- What are the blockers to implementing payments today?
- What would need to happen for you to adopt this?

#### 6. Decision Process (5 minutes)
- Who makes the decision to adopt new payment infrastructure?
- What's the approval process?
- What are the key decision factors?
- Who else would be involved in the decision?

### Closing (3 minutes)
"Thanks for your time. This is really helpful. Would you be interested in being an early adopter? We're offering [incentive - e.g., free tier, 50% off first year, co-marketing]. I'll send you a follow-up email with next steps."
