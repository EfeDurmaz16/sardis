**Send timing:** Tuesday through Thursday, 9-11am recipient local time.

**Send order:** Follow the numbered order. Wave 1A first (accounts 1-10), then 1B (11-18) after initial response data.

**For Wave 1B larger companies (Ramp, Zip, Mercury, Airwallex, Fireblocks):** Strongly consider leading with the secondary buyer persona instead of the CEO. The person who feels the governance gap daily will care more than the founder who is thinking about 50 other priorities.

**Follow-up cadence:** Day 3, Day 7, Day 14 (see playbook for templates).

**Tracking:** Use the first-wave-ops.xlsx to track send status, reply status, and next steps per account.
