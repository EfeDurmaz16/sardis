## Format Guide

Every email follows the refined format:
- **Subject:** Sardis // [personalized hook]
- **Opener:** Personalized to their thesis, portfolio, or recent writing
- **Problem:** Agents cannot safely spend money
- **Solution:** One line on Sardis
- **Traction:** Bullet points (scannable)
- **Competitive edge + raise**
- **CTA:** Specific hook, not generic "15 minutes"
- **Sign-off:** Best, Efe Baran Durmaz / Founder, Sardis | sardis.sh

No em-dashes. No double hyphens. No feature lists. Problem first, value always.
