# Sardis Outreach Email Rewrites
**For Review — March 2026**

## What changed and why

Every draft had these problems:
- Em-dashes in subjects ("Sardis + X — tagline") — spammy, generic
- No paragraph breaks — hard to skim
- Too long — buried the ask
- Value was Sardis-centric, not company-specific

These rewrites are:
- 3-5 sentences max
- Specific to what each company actually does
- No em-dashes anywhere
- Clear one-line ask at the end

## Audience note

The target list is correct. Two clear buckets:
1. **Agent infra** (E2B, Skyvern, CrewAI, LangChain, MultiOn, Composio, Relevance AI, Dust, Parallel, Stacks) — pitch is a developer integration so their users can add payments to agents
2. **Finance AI** (Stampli, Airbase, Ramp, Xelix, Procure.ai, Lio, Sierra, Decagon) — pitch is closing the execution gap their AI currently has

Both are high-intent buyers at seed stage.
