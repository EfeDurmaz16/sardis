# Sardis Investor Cold Emails
**For Review | March 2026 | Seed Round ($1-3M)**

## Email Philosophy

Based on YC, a16z, and Sequoia fundraising guides:

- **Problem first.** What breaks today. Not what Sardis does.
- **Traction up front.** 25k+ SDK installs in 3 weeks. AutoGPT and Helicone committed. 15+ framework integrations. Merchant checkout live.
- **No feature lists.** Every sentence earns its place.
- **Personalized opener.** Reference their thesis, recent deal, or portfolio company.
- **Clear ask.** 15 minutes. Link to deck.
- **Under 200 words.** Respect their inbox.

## Traction one-liner (used in emails)

"25,000+ SDK installs in our first three weeks, AutoGPT and Helicone committed as our first design partners, 15+ agent framework integrations live, and a merchant checkout flow already in production."

## Competitive context (used where relevant)

Competitors with less traction and less technical depth have raised $10M+. Sardis has the product, the integrations, and the early customers. We need capital to launch virtual card programs (capital-intensive, like Lithic), hire GTM, and build our compliance team.
