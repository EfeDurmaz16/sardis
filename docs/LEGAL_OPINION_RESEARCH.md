## Request for Proposal (RFP)

### RFP Template

**Subject:** Legal Opinion Request - Non-Custodial Payment Infrastructure

**Dear [Law Firm],**

We are Sardis, a payment infrastructure platform for AI agents. We are seeking a legal opinion on our non-custodial architecture and compliance requirements.

**Company Overview:**
- **Product:** Non-custodial Agent Wallet & Payment OS
- **Architecture:** MPC-based wallets, no fund custody
- **Services:** Payment execution, policy enforcement, mandate verification
- **Target Market:** AI agents, API providers, agent frameworks

**Scope of Work:**
We need a legal opinion addressing:

1. **MSB Registration:**
   - Does our non-custodial architecture exempt us from FinCEN MSB registration?
   - What are the criteria for MSB registration?
   - What activities would trigger MSB registration?

2. **State MTL Requirements:**
   - State-by-state analysis of MTL requirements
   - Which states require MTL for our business model?
   - What are the exemptions (if any)?

3. **AML Monitoring:**
   - Are we required to implement AML monitoring even if non-custodial?
   - What are the obligations for payment processors vs. infrastructure providers?
   - What level of monitoring is required?

4. **KYC Requirements:**
   - Does agent identity verification constitute KYC?
   - What are the KYC requirements for our business model?
   - What level of identity verification is required?

5. **Cross-Border Compliance:**
   - OFAC sanctions screening requirements
   - Cross-border payment compliance
   - International regulatory considerations

6. **Tax Reporting:**
   - 1099-K reporting requirements
   - Tax obligations for payment processors
   - State tax considerations

**Deliverables:**
- Written legal opinion (15-30 pages)
- Compliance roadmap
- Risk assessment
- Recommendations

**Timeline:**
- Proposal due: [Date]
- Engagement start: [Date]
- Opinion delivery: 4-6 weeks from engagement

**Budget:**
- Target: $10-20K
- Please provide detailed cost breakdown

**Questions:**
Please contact [Your Name] at [Email] or [Phone].

Thank you for your consideration.

## Key Questions for Lawyer

### MSB Registration
1. Does non-custodial architecture exempt us from FinCEN MSB registration?
2. What activities would trigger MSB registration?
3. What are the criteria for "money transmission"?
4. Are we a "money transmitter" or "payment processor"?

### State MTL Requirements
1. Which states require MTL for our business model?
2. What are the exemptions (if any)?
3. What's the cost and timeline for MTL in each state?
4. Can we operate without MTL in certain states?

### AML Monitoring
1. Are we required to implement AML monitoring even if non-custodial?
2. What level of monitoring is required?
3. What are the obligations for payment processors vs. infrastructure providers?
4. Do we need to file SARs (Suspicious Activity Reports)?

### KYC Requirements
1. Does agent identity verification constitute KYC?
2. What level of identity verification is required?
3. What are the KYC requirements for our business model?
4. Do we need full KYC or is "light KYC" sufficient?

### Cross-Border Compliance
1. What are the OFAC sanctions screening requirements?
2. What are the cross-border payment compliance requirements?
3. What are the international regulatory considerations?
4. Do we need licenses in other countries?

### Tax Reporting
1. Are we required to file 1099-K forms?
2. What are the tax obligations for payment processors?
3. What are the state tax considerations?
4. What are the international tax considerations?
