Draft a design partner outreach email for $ARGUMENTS.

Steps:
1. Research the company using WebSearch
2. Understand what their AI agents do and where payments fit
3. Identify the specific payment gap Sardis fills for them
4. Draft a concise email (under 150 words)

The email should:
- Lead with THEIR problem, not Sardis features
- Show you understand their product
- Explain how Sardis solves their specific payment gap
- Mention "no cost to you, we need a real use case"
- End with a clear ask (15 min call this week)
