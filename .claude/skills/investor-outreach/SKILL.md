Draft an investor outreach email for $ARGUMENTS.

Steps:
1. Research the investor/fund using WebSearch
2. Find their investment thesis and portfolio companies
3. Identify the connection to Sardis (fintech, AI infra, payments, crypto)
4. Draft a concise email (under 150 words)
5. Include a personalized subject line

- Reference something specific about their portfolio or thesis
- Explain Sardis in one sentence
- Mention 1-2 traction points (MPP/Stripe access, 50K installs)
- End with a clear ask (15 min call)
- No emdashes, no emojis, no hype
