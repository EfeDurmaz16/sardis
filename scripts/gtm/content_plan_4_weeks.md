# Sardis GTM Content Plan (4 Weeks)

## Positioning

- Core narrative: `Policy-controlled financial actions for AI agents`
- Tone: Paul-Graham-like in email (clear, direct, specific)
- Distribution channels:
  - X (questions + build logs)
  - Reddit (helpful technical responses)
  - Cold email (founder-to-founder)

## Weekly Cadence

- X: 5 posts/week (3 question posts, 2 insight/build posts)
- Reddit: 3 high-quality comments/week
- Email: 20-40 personalized emails/week
- Follow-ups: 2 touches (day 3, day 7)

## KPI Targets (First 4 Weeks)

- 300-500 new leads collected
- 80+ leads scored `hot`
- 120-160 emails queued
- 8-15 positive replies
- 4-8 intro calls booked

---

## Week 1 - Discovery + Narrative Baseline

### Goals

- Validate top pain points in agent spending workflows
- Establish Sardis as "trust/control" layer

### X Posts (5)

1. `If an AI agent can spend money, what should the approval model look like?`
2. `Where does agent identity live in your stack today: key, wallet, or policy layer?`
3. Build log: `What we learned from testnet traction before mainnet`
4. `How are teams auditing agent actions right now?`
5. Insight: `Autonomy without auditability is chaos with confidence.`

### Reddit (3)

- Respond in r/ArtificialIntelligence on agent guardrails
- Respond in r/programming on deterministic policy engines
- Respond in r/SaaS on AI spend control patterns

### Email (20-30)

- Segment: AI infra, agent frameworks, LLM tooling
- CTA: 15-minute architecture call

## Week 2 - Mainnet Readiness Narrative

### Goals

- Convert curiosity to concrete calls
- Highlight "fail-closed" execution model

### X Posts (5)

1. `Should agent actions be event-sourced for auditability?`
2. Build log: `Fail-closed checks we enforce before execution`
3. `What is your rollback strategy when an agent misfires?`
4. `Do you use merchant allowlists for autonomous spend?`
5. Insight: `Capability scales fast. Control has to scale faster.`

### Reddit (3)

- Technical comment on policy + approval workflows
- Technical comment on stablecoin-to-fiat operational flow
- Technical comment on multi-agent coordination risk

### Email (30-40)

- Send to hot leads from week 1 + fresh top-scored leads
- Include one practical question based on lead context

## Week 3 - Social Proof + Design Partner Motion

### Goals

- Push design-partner pipeline forward
- Build confidence around practical integrations

### X Posts (5)

### Email (30-40)

### Goals

### Reddit (3)

### Email (40)
