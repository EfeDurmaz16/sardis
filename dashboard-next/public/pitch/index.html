The Problem
Giving an AI Agent a Credit Card is Dangerous
AI agents are becoming autonomous workers that need to spend real money. But today, there is no trust layer between agents and financial systems.
Agents retry failed payments 100x. They confuse $1.00 with $100.00. They drift from their task and spend on unrelated things. Traditional payment controls were designed for humans — not machines.

Retry Loops

Agent retries a failed call 100x, turning $10 into $1,000

Decimal Errors

Sends $100.00 instead of $1.00 — one decimal mistake

Task Drift

Goes off-track and spends on things it was never told to buy

Zero Visibility

No per-agent audit trail — can't track who spent what
